Overview

Purpose

I have taken the huge amounts of time to watch all of campaign one and two as well as read the aforementioned books and have taken *many* notes. On this site I will have all the lore we know thus far sorted in easy to navigate pages, separated and put as straightforward as possible. I am going into the creation of this with the mindset that users may have little to no knowledge about Critical Role’s setting let alone DnD rules and common terms. My hope is to help new critters feel comfortable in their knowledge and not feel like they need to devote a large amount of time to watch the previous campaigns like I did. This should give them all the resources they need to feel comfortable and informed on their own time.

Audience

New or Old Crtitical Role fans

Normal paragraph example

Critical Role is a long-running web series in which our group of nerdy voice actors use tabletop roleplaying game mechanics as the medium to build a longform improvisational story in the vast and rich fantasy world of Exandria. The players are guided through the world by Matthew Mercer, an incredibly experienced Game Master. Essentially, this story is like a four-hour long improvised episode of television, with each campaign focusing on its own set of characters within the world.
